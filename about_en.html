About M3U8 Player

M3U8 Player is a lightweight and user-friendly online video player based on the Tencent Cloud SDK. It supports playback of `.m3u8`, HLS, and MP4 video formats using modern HTML5 technology.

Our goal is to provide developers, testers, and users with an easy-to-use web tool for previewing or testing M3U8 streaming URLs.

Key Features:

  • Supports live and on-demand streaming
  • Responsive design for both mobile and desktop
  • Powered by Tencent’s TCPlayer video engine
